Steel Plate

  • One cubic foot of steel plate = 490 pounds.
  • 490/12 = 40.8lbs/sq.ft per 1in thick.
  • 40.8lbs/sq.ft per 1in thick is rounded off to 40lbs/sq.ft per 1in thick for convenience.
  • Every 1/8in of plate 1 sq.ft = 5lbs
  • 1/8in thick = 5lbs/sq.ft
  • 1/4in thick = 10lbs/sq.ft
  • 3/8in thick = 15lbs/sq.ft
  • 1/2in thick = 20lbs/sq.ft
  • 5/8in thick = 25lbs/sq.ft
  • 3/4in thick = 30lbs/sq.ft
  • 7/8in thick = 35lbs/sq.ft
  • 1in thick = 40lbs/sq.ft
